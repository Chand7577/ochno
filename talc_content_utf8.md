## Section 5 ΓÇö Industry applications 

Talc serves more industries than almost any other mineral\. Each vertical has entirely different technical requirements ΓÇö the page addresses each one directly\.

- Cosmetics & personal care \(EU/USA/Japan ΓÇö highest value segment\): talc as base in face powder, foundation, eyeshadow, blush, baby powder; valued for softness \(Mohs 1\), lubricity, oil absorption, skin slip; key buyer concerns: heavy metals asbestos\-free certificate, particle size for skin feel, ethical sourcing declaration
- Pharmaceuticals \(IP/BP/USP grade\): tablet lubricant and glidant ΓÇö reduces friction in tablet presses; dusting powder for surgical gloves; excipient in topical formulations; strict requirements: pharmacopoeia test reports, heavy metals, arsenic, lead limits, microbial limits
- Plastics & polymer compounds: talc nucleates crystallisation in PP ΓÇö improves stiffness \(flexural modulus\), heat deflection temperature, reduces warpage; used in automotive PP bumpers, dashboards, appliance housings; platy morphology \(high aspect ratio\) is critical; surface\-treated grade improves matrix adhesion in nylon/PP
- Paints & coatings: functional extender ΓÇö improves barrier resistance, reduces mud\-cracking, provides matt finish; platy particles orient parallel to surface creating a physical moisture barrier; used in primers, anti\-corrosion coatings, interior emulsions
- Ceramics & sanitaryware: lowers firing temperature, improves whiteness, controls thermal expansion coefficient ΓÇö used in wall tile bodies, sanitaryware, and technical ceramics \(cordierite, steatite\)
- Paper industry: filler \(improves smoothness, opacity, printability\) and coating pigment ΓÇö platy morphology fills surface roughness; reduces ink absorption variation
- Rubber & sealants: anti\-tack agent, reinforcing filler; in sealants improves thixotropy and reduces slump; stearic acid coated grade preferred
